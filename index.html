Mortgage Payment Calculator

This web app calculates the monthly mortgage payment for a fixed-rate mortgage.

Enter the principal, the yearly rate as a percentage, and select the length of the mortgage. After that, click the calculate button to see the monthly mortgage payment for a fixed-rate mortgage.
